Understanding Skin Types

Dry Skin

Dry skin often feels tight and may appear flaky or rough. It requires hydration and gentle care to maintain its balance.

Oily Skin

Oily skin is characterized by excess sebum production, leading to a shiny appearance and potential for acne. Proper cleansing and lightweight moisturizers are key.

Combination Skin

Combination skin features both dry and oily areas, typically with an oily T-zone and drier cheeks. A balanced skincare routine is essential.

Normal Skin

Normal skin is well-balanced, with minimal imperfections. Maintaining this balance with a consistent routine is important.

Sensitive Skin

Sensitive skin is prone to redness and irritation. Gentle, fragrance-free products are recommended to soothe and protect it.
