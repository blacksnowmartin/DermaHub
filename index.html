Frequently Asked Questions

What is Derma Hub?

Derma Hub is an AI-powered platform that provides personalized skincare recommendations based on your unique skin type and concerns.

How does the AI analysis work?

Our AI uses advanced algorithms to analyze your skin's characteristics from uploaded images, helping to identify your skin type and specific needs.

Are the product recommendations unbiased?

Yes, our recommendations are based purely on your skin's needs and scientific data, not on brand partnerships or promotions.

Can I track my skin's progress over time?

Absolutely! Derma Hub allows you to log your routine and track changes in your skin, helping you see the effectiveness of your personalized regimen.
